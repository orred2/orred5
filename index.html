⚙ Interactive Assembly Guide
1Printer Body
2Cassette
3Screws
4Final Check
🖨️
Position the Printer Body
Set the WF-C5890 main body on a flat, stable surface with the front panel facing you. Ensure all shipping tape and packaging foam has been removed.
  • Place on a flat, level surface
  • Remove all shipping tape & foam
  • Confirm ink lock tab is visible on the left side
  • Unlock the ink lock tab before proceeding
📄
Install Cassette 1
Slide Cassette 1 (C1) into the front lower slot of the printer. Load up to 250 sheets of plain paper before inserting if desired.
  • Adjust the paper guides to match your paper size
  • Load paper short-edge first, printable side up
  • Do not exceed the MAX fill line
  • Slide cassette in until it clicks firmly into place
🔩
Secure with Screws
If installing the optional cabinet or additional paper tray, attach using the provided screws. Use a #2 Phillips screwdriver — do not overtighten.
  • Align cabinet/tray mounting holes first
  • Hand-tighten all screws before final torque
  • Tighten in a cross pattern for even seating
  • Check that unit does not rock after tightening
✅
Final Assembly Check
Before powering on, confirm all components are seated correctly and the printer is ready for initial ink charging.
  • Ink lock tab is in the UNLOCKED position
  • Cassette is fully inserted and level
  • Output tray extender pulled out (if needed)
  • Power cable connected — power on and follow LCD prompts
